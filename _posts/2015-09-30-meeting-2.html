Our second meeting is on this Friday, October 2nd at 5:00 in HRBB 113. Our talk this meeting will be over deep learning neural networks and will be followed by a very interesting video from Stanford regarding deep learning. We will also discuss SIGAI membership, its benefits and several other resources relating to A.I. that are available to everyone. We will also discuss our current RoboCode programming competition and allow time for general discussion and comparing agents. Please join us for an insight into A.I.!
